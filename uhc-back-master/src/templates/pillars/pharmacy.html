Pharmacy

At UHC Staffing, we specialize in placing skilled nurses across all clinical settings — from critical care and emergency departments to med-surg and long-term care. Our credentialing process is Joint Commission-aligned, ensuring every RN, LPN, and LVN meets strict licensure, training, and facility-specific EMR requirements. We support hospitals, outpatient centers, and skilled Pharmacy facilities in maintaining safe staffing ratios, boosting patient satisfaction, and minimizing burnout through timely, compliant placements.

Benefits

Our pharmacy professionals receive support designed for accuracy-driven, compliance-heavy roles. We offer competitive compensation, CEUs and licensing assistance, flexible contracts, and access to roles that match your therapeutic expertise and workflow preference. Whether you're filling in during peak demand or seeking a permanent placement, our team ensures you stay credentialed, supported, and connected to organizations that value pharmacy as clinical care, not just a back-office function.
